Structural & Miscelleneous Steel Detailing

Structural and Miscellaneous Steel Detailing is a specialized engineering service that transforms design concepts into precise fabrication and erection drawings for steel structures. This process involves creating detailed shop drawings, 3D models, and material lists for primary structural elements (beams, columns, trusses) as well as secondary components (stairs, railings, platforms, and custom connections).

Using advanced software like Tekla Structures, AutoCAD, and Revit, steel detailers ensure compliance with AISC, AWS, and ISO standards while optimizing material usage and minimizing construction errors. The service plays a vital role in commercial buildings, industrial plants, bridges, and infrastructure projects by providing accurate documentation that guides fabricators and erectors, reducing costs and timelines while improving structural integrity and safety. Professional steel detailing bridges the gap between engineering design and physical construction, ensuring seamless execution from workshop to job site.

Bridge Detailing

Bridge Steel Detailing is a highly specialized engineering service that converts structural designs into precise fabrication and erection drawings for bridge construction. This meticulous process involves creating detailed shop drawings for girders, piers, bearings, and expansion joints, along with 3D modeling of complex connections and seismic reinforcements. Using advanced software like Tekla Structures, AutoCAD Civil 3D, and MIDAS, bridge detailers account for dynamic loads, wind forces, and thermal expansion while complying with AASHTO, DOT, and EN standards.

The service covers all bridge types - from beam and arch bridges to cable-stayed and suspension structures - ensuring accurate documentation for fabricators and construction teams. By optimizing material usage and identifying potential clashes in the virtual stage, bridge detailing significantly reduces construction risks, enhances durability, and accelerates project timelines while meeting stringent safety requirements for critical infrastructure projects.

Connection Design and Stamping

Connection Design and Stamping is the engineering backbone of steel construction, where we transform structural concepts into buildable, high-performance joints. Our team specializes in crafting intelligent connections that balance strength with practical constructability—from sleek moment frames in skyscrapers to robust bracing systems for industrial plants. Using advanced modeling tools, we visualize load paths and stress distributions to create connections that work seamlessly with your fabrication process.

Every design undergoes rigorous review before our licensed engineers provide certified stamped drawings, complete with clear welding and bolting instructions. We go beyond basic compliance to develop innovative connection solutions that save time on-site, reduce steel tonnage, and prevent costly field modifications.

Whether it's a complex architectural node or standard beam-to-column joints, our detail-oriented approach ensures your steel structure comes together precisely as intended—with safety and efficiency built into every connection.

Estimation Support Services

Estimation Support Services provide critical cost intelligence for steel construction projects, delivering accurate material and labor forecasts before fabrication begins. Our team transforms structural drawings into detailed quantity takeoffs and budget analyses, identifying every beam, plate, and connection point to prevent costly oversights.

We leverage historical data and real-time market pricing to generate reliable estimates that account for material fluctuations, fabrication complexity, and regional labor rates. From preliminary conceptual budgets to bid-ready proposals, our estimating specialists help contractors and fabricators sharpen their competitive edge—flagging potential cost drivers early while optimizing steel utilization.

Whether you need a rapid conceptual estimate or a full breakdown with alternate design comparisons, we deliver the financial clarity needed to make informed decisions at every project stage.

Client Specific Detaling Automation

Client-Specific Detailing Automation revolutionizes steel fabrication by transforming your unique project requirements into intelligent, self-executing workflows. We develop customized automation scripts that mirror your company's detailing standards, drawing conventions, and fabrication preferences—turning repetitive tasks into single-click operations.

Our solutions integrate directly with your existing Tekla, AutoCAD, or Revit environment to automatically generate connection details, apply standardized callouts, and enforce your QA/QC protocols. By capturing your best practices in executable algorithms, we eliminate manual errors while accelerating output by 40-70%.

The system learns from your historical projects to suggest optimal detailing approaches, automatically flags deviations from your specs, and even adapts to different client or project-specific requirements.

From automated shop drawing generation to AI-assisted clash detection tuned to your typical steel assemblies, we engineer efficiency that grows smarter with each project—putting your competitive advantage on autopilot.

Decking Support & Detailing

Decking Support & Detailing provides precision-engineered solutions for metal deck and composite floor systems, ensuring optimal performance from installation through final construction. Our team delivers shop-ready drawings that coordinate deck profiles, embeds, and penetrations with structural steel framing and MEP systems, eliminating field conflicts before they occur.

We specialize in complex multi-level deck layouts, accounting for cantilevers, openings, and unique architectural requirements while maximizing material efficiency. Our detailing includes precise welding/anchoring details, shear stud placement maps, and pour stop configurations tailored to your concrete specifications.

By integrating decking with the primary steel model early in the process, we prevent costly resequencing during erection and ensure seamless collaboration between steel erectors and concrete crews—all while maintaining strict compliance with manufacturer specs and load-bearing requirements.

Stick Modelling & Take Offs

Stick Modeling & Takeoffs streamline the pre-fabrication process by creating simplified structural models that capture all essential steel components without complex detailing. Our team develops accurate stick models that serve as the backbone for preliminary material quantification, cost estimation, and erection sequencing.

These lightweight models allow for rapid visualization of the structural skeleton while maintaining critical dimensional data for beams, columns, and bracing systems. From these models, we generate precise material takeoffs that include weight calculations, piece counts, and length optimizations - providing contractors and fabricators with reliable data for bidding and procurement.

The process identifies potential clashes early, optimizes steel ordering quantities, and establishes a clear framework for subsequent detailed modeling, significantly reducing rework and material waste during later project stages.
